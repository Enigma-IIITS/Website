CLUB ENIGMA

Club Enigma is the distinguished research-funded technical club at the Indian Institute of Information Technology Sri-City (IIITS) that is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology through practical, hands-on projects. At Enigma, we believe in the power of doing, and our primary focus is on Computer Graphics, Cybersecurity, Distributed Systems, and System Software.
